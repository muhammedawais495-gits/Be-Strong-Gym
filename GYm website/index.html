About Us

Be Strong Gym was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where everyone, regardless of their fitness level, could come together to achieve their health and wellness goals. What started as a small facility with just a few pieces of equipment has grown into one of Islamabad's premier fitness destinations.

Our founder, inspired by personal transformation through fitness, recognized the need for a gym that wasn't just about building muscles, but about building character, discipline, and a supportive community. Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of members who trust us with their fitness journey.

At Be Strong Gym, we believe that strength comes in many forms – physical, mental, and emotional. Our mission is to empower you with the tools, guidance, and motivation you need to become the strongest version of yourself.

Terms & Conditions

1. Membership Policy

All memberships require a one-time admission fee of Rs. 2,000 for first-time members. Membership is non-transferable and strictly for personal use only.

2. Payment Terms

Monthly memberships must be paid in advance by the 1st of each month. Package fees are to be paid in full at the time of enrollment. No refunds will be issued for unused membership periods.

3. Facility Usage

Members must strictly adhere to designated timing hours for men (2 PM - 11 PM) and women (8 AM - 1 PM). Proper gym attire and clean athletic shoes are mandatory. Members must return all equipment to designated areas after use.

4. Health & Safety

Members must disclose any pre-existing medical conditions or injuries before starting their fitness program. Be Strong Gym is not liable for any injuries sustained during workouts. First-time members are encouraged to consult with our trainers.

5. Code of Conduct

Respectful behavior towards staff and fellow members is mandatory. Use of mobile phones is restricted to designated areas. Photography or videography inside the gym premises is strictly prohibited without prior written permission.

6. Personal Belongings

While personal lockers are provided, the gym is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged personal items. Members are advised to use locks on their assigned lockers.

7. Suspension & Termination

The management reserves the right to suspend or terminate membership for violation of gym rules, misconduct, or non-payment of fees without refund.

8. Modifications

Be Strong Gym reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions, membership fees, or facility timings with prior notice to members.

By enrolling as a member of Be Strong Gym, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by all terms and conditions.
